Pizza

An Italian Pizza

A delicious Italian Pizza done with a homemade touch from Verona, Italy

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Garlic Oil
  • 1/4 cup tomato sauce, divided
  • 1 cup chopped tomatoes, divided
  • 1/4 cup roasted red peppers, dranied and chopped, divided
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh basil, divided

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Place dough in a well-oiled bowl and cover with a damp cloth.
  3. Set aside to rise until doubled, about 1 hour. Punch down; knead in garlic and basil. Set aside to rise for 1 more hour, or until doubled again.
  4. Meanwhile, make garlic oil: Combine 1/2 cup olive oil with minced garlic in a microwave-safe cup or bowl. Heat for 30 seconds in the microwave.
  5. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat; brush the grate with garlic oil.
  6. Working quickly, brush garlic oil over crust.
  7. Top with 1/2 of each of the following: tomato sauce, chopped tomatoes, olives, red peppers, cheese, and basil.
  8. Close the lid and cook until cheese melts. Remove from grill and set aside to cool for a few minutes. Repeat with second piece of dough.
